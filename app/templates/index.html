Rules: In Ghost50, everyone is a winner!*
Players go in a circle, submitting one letter at a time, forming a growing word fragment. You lose if:
1) the letter you entered forms a 4-letter or longer word
2) the letter you type cannot be built upon to form a valid word
3) the time you had to enter a letter runs out.

*Except the loser.
